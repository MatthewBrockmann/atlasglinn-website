Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Atlas Glinn, LLC ("Atlas Glinn," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Atlas EP iOS application ("App") and related services. By using Atlas EP, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Who We Are

Atlas Glinn, LLC is a Houston, Texas–based security technology company. Atlas EP is our AI-powered executive protection platform built for professional security operators, protection agents, and high-value principals.

Contact: privacy@atlasglinn.com | 2450 Fondren Rd, Suite 255, Houston, TX 77063

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an Atlas EP account, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Job title and organizational role (Agent, Operator, Command, etc.)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Team and organizational affiliations

2.2 Location Data

Atlas EP collects precise GPS location data to enable core features including Blue Force Tracker, route monitoring, and emergency response coordination. Location collection occurs:

  • In the foreground when the App is actively in use
  • In the background during active operational deployments (requires explicit permission)

You may disable background location access at any time in iOS Settings; however, this will disable real-time team tracking features.

2.3 Communications Data

Atlas EP transmits encrypted operational messages, voice communications, and status updates between authorized team members. All communications are end-to-end encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. We do not read or analyze the content of your communications.

2.4 Device & Technical Data

  • Device model, iOS version, and unique device identifiers
  • App version and crash/diagnostic data
  • Network connectivity information
  • Push notification tokens

2.5 Health & Biometric Data

With your explicit permission, Atlas EP may access heart rate and motion data via Apple HealthKit and Apple Watch to support operator wellness monitoring. This data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to our servers without your consent.

2.6 Emergency & Incident Data

When SOS or emergency features are activated, Atlas EP collects and transmits your location, device status, and incident details to designated emergency contacts and team members as configured in your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core services — team coordination, tracking, encrypted communications, threat monitoring
  • Emergency response — transmitting location and status to your authorized team during SOS events
  • Account management — authentication, profile management, team configuration
  • Security — fraud prevention, abuse detection, and enforcement of our Terms of Service
  • App improvement — anonymized crash reports and performance analytics
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law

We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Within Your Team

Atlas EP is a collaborative platform. Your name, role, status, and location (when location sharing is enabled) are visible to other authorized members of your operational team.

4.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating the App, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and real-time database infrastructure)
  • Cloudflare (network infrastructure and DDoS protection)
  • Apple (push notifications via APNs, HealthKit integration)
  • Stripe (payment processing — see Section 7)
  • Twilio (SMS and voice communications)
  • Anthropic (AI-powered threat analysis features)

All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when necessary to protect the safety of individuals or prevent criminal activity.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections described herein.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Operational logs and communications are retained for 90 days by default; you may configure shorter retention periods in your account settings. Upon account deletion, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

Atlas EP implements industry-leading security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all data at rest and in transit
  • WireGuard VPN for secure network communications
  • Zero-trust architecture — data is verified at every access point
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

While we take every precaution to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We will notify affected users promptly in the event of a data breach as required by applicable law.

7. Payments

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. Atlas Glinn does not store your full payment card information. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy. In-app purchases (if applicable) are processed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Atlas EP is intended for professional use by adults (18 years of age or older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at privacy@atlasglinn.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of non-essential data processing
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw previously granted consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@atlasglinn.com. We will respond within 30 days.

iOS Permissions

You may manage Atlas EP's access to device features (location, camera, microphone, health data, notifications) at any time in iOS Settings → Atlas EP.
