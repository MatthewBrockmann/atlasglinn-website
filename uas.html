Autonomous Drones That Never Sleep

Sunflower Labs partnership delivering fully autonomous aerial surveillance

Atlas Glinn partners with Sunflower Labs to deliver cutting-edge autonomous drone technology integrated into complete security solutions. The Beehive system deploys The Bee — a fully autonomous drone — on demand or on schedule, with zero human intervention required.

AI detection identifies people, vehicles, and animals with real-time tracking. The drone autonomously navigates, surveys, deters, and returns to base to recharge — all without a pilot. This is security that never takes a break.
