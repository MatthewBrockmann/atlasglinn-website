Counter-Drone Solutions

AirWarden by AeroDefense — The Only System That Simultaneously Locates Both Drone AND Pilot

Atlas Glinn is the Texas regional partner for AeroDefense, bringing cutting-edge counter-drone technology to critical infrastructure, high-profile events, and security-sensitive facilities. AirWarden simultaneously locates both the drone and its pilot, enabling security and law enforcement personnel to quickly address the source of potential threats — not just the drone in the air.

Made in the USA, AirWarden provides real-time early warning and situational awareness for critical infrastructure, public safety, and national security applications. All solutions are FAA-compliant, ensuring lawful and effective deployment.
